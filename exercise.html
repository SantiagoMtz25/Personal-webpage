I have always exercised in my life. I started playing tenis, then I switched to football for a couple of years, until I discoverd swimming which I fell in love with I went on and swam for more than 8 years. I used to compete and even though, I was not the best at it, it helped me to develop a responsability towards exercise and correct nutrition for myself. During Highshcool I neglected for certain periods exercising, from now and then I would start exercising but soon after I would abandoned it. After Highshcool I spent a year trying to figure out my life in which I did not exercised. It was not until my second semester of university when I strongly commited to the gym, the previous year had been the most complicated one in my life, and I was at my worst physical form, and I hit rock bottom. I decide to turn that situation around, so I established a nutrition and training plan for me. And I stuck with it for a year. I went from weighing 62kg to 79kg at my max weight. Today, I am weighing aroung 75kg, and I could not feel better or happier with my own body.

March 2022

Weight: 62kg

When I started I did not feel confortable in my own body. I regularly would recieve comments about how skinny I was; even though, they were not bad intentioned, they hurt. I took action about it and here is where all started.
